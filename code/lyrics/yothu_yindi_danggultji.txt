Genre: International
Subgenres: [u'Aboriginal Rock', u'Worldbeat', u'Ambient']

Song:

Song from the Rirratjingu Clan of NE Arnhem Land
It describes Dhanbul people. This song is about survival and defending
Territory while we are alive. It describes Gudurrku (a large bird) which is
Used in the song as a metaphore. When we die our spirits fly to Garrtjpirr
Burralgu
